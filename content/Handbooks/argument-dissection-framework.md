:::{.margin}
I formulated this framework after finding myself accepting the claims made in a piece of writing too passively. I read and nod along, but find that nothing really sticks.

This helps
:::

| Item                 | **Sharpening Lens**                                                           | **Purpose**                                                      | **Core Question**                                          | **When to Use It**                                               |
| -------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Definition           |                                                                               | Stabilize meaning                                                | What is this thing?                                        | When terms are fuzzy, overloaded, or people talk past each other |
| Central Tenet        | “Most people think X is the problem, but this view claims the real issue is…” | Name what really matters<br><br>  <br><br>**Commits**            | What is the core claim everything depends on?              | When identifying the load-bearing idea                           |
| Corollary            | “In practice, this would manifest as…”                                        | Expand truth cleanly<br><br>  <br><br>**Litmus tests**           | If the tenet is true, what logically follows?              | When extending the idea without adding opinion                   |
| Implication          | “This reframes a common belief: X is not actually about Y, but about…”        | Expose pressure & responsibility<br><br>  <br><br>**Interprets** | What does this force us to confront in the real world?     | When incentives, constraints, or tensions appear                 |
| Bridge / Translation | “The cost of ignoring this shows up as…”                                      | Help the idea travel                                             | How does this idea become legible to a different audience? | When translating across roles, power, or incentives              |
| Practice             | “If we took this seriously, we would stop…”                                   | Change day-to-day action                                         | What do we do differently because of this?                 | When behavior, habits, or questions change                       |
